Since 2018, Nihongo Sekai has been connecting learners worldwide with the beauty and depth of Japanese language and culture. We believe that language learning is a bridge to understanding, friendship, and global connection.

Our Mission

To make Japanese language learning accessible, engaging, and culturally enriching for students around the world. We combine traditional teaching methods with modern technology to create meaningful learning experiences.

Our Vision

To become the world's leading platform for Japanese language and cultural education, fostering global understanding and creating lasting connections between Japan and the world.

Our Values

Respect for culture, commitment to excellence, personalized learning, and building authentic relationships between students and teachers. We honor Japanese traditions while embracing innovation.

Our Story

2018

The Beginning

Founded in Tokyo by Hiroshi Tanaka and Sarah Kim, two language educators passionate about connecting cultures through Japanese language learning.

2019

First 1,000 Students

Reached our first milestone with students from 25 countries, proving the demand for quality online Japanese education.

2021

Global Expansion

Opened our San Francisco office and launched live classroom features, enabling real-time cultural exchange between students and native speakers.

2024

Today

Serving over 50,000 students globally with 200+ certified teachers and innovative AI-powered learning tools, while maintaining our commitment to cultural authenticity.
