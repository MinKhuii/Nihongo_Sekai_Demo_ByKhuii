Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly do you respond to inquiries?

We typically respond to emails within 24 hours during business days. Live chat and phone support are available for urgent matters.

Can I schedule a demo or trial lesson?

Yes! Contact us to schedule a free 30-minute trial lesson with one of our certified teachers. This helps you experience our teaching style before committing to a course.

Do you offer corporate training programs?

We provide customized Japanese training programs for businesses. Contact our partnerships team to discuss your company's specific needs.

What languages do you provide support in?

Our support team is available in English, Japanese, Mandarin, Korean, and Spanish. Please specify your preferred language when contacting us.
